Informations about our Restaurant

Whether you’re a CrossFit gym, yoga studio or offer group fitness classes, you know that a healthy diet is as important to your clients asthe hard work they put into their workouts. With your members’ busy lives, sticking to a healthy diet is probably one of their biggest challenges. They may not have the time to cook a healthy meal or meal prep each week.

Imagine being able to offer your clients this critical ingredient to optimal health. Your members have a killer workout, and grab a day or a week’s worth of healthy meals. Diet’n Shake delivers healthy, pre- cooked and measured ready-to-eat meals to your gym each week for members to pick up and enjoy, supplying the perfect fuel to power them through their next workout.

Diet and Shake is a startup by young entrepreneurs with a mission of embracing the health of people while sufficing their taste buds, today, when we are all busy, working, we absolutely pay downright ignorance towards our health, without realizing that we are putting it in danger, today when 30 percent of the world is suffering from obesity, and rest 70 percent people having minor to major health issues.

Having a mentor always makes things easy, doesn't it? So here we give you a diet mentor, Diet and Shake. We prepare food not only with the lure of keeping it healthy but we also ensure that the food has the capability to replace the delicious chicken biryani you had sometimes back. We grow our own fruits & vegetables by the hydroponic technology, providing them with additional minerals. We also have a poultry farm, where we ensure that the place is clean enough and that the chicken is healthy enough to be consumed by our customers.

Often we have been running oppositely with diets. Alas! No more extra-cheesy white sauce Italian pasta that you adored, no more pastries to suffice that sweet tooth, but on a scale of 1-10 tell us how happy you would be if we manifest that we can provide the meal which can be healthy and delicious at the same time, we experimented and proved the “If you want to lose weight, forget what the word delicious means” wrong. We put a stamp on our meals everyday, with a date tag to ensure our customers and gaining their trusts eventually. Diet and Shakes makes a symbiotic relation with its customers ensuring that their demands are fulfilled in the span of time they provided us with.

“Don’t be shy, come try.” Bon Appétit.
